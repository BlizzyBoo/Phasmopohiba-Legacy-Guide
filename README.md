## Why Can' I Access Legacy Versions By Normal Means?
The reason behind Kinectic making downgrading your game via normal means not possible is due to royality rights of assets, royalties are what the developer pays in % per a copy of the game sold. So making it impossible by normal means to downgrade and updating the maps, it will remove the need for kinetic to pay royalties to the authors of the assets used in older versions. Originally Phasmophobia was developed by Daniel Knight and it is common for developers to pay to designers for assets. Downgrading is offically onyl allowed by developer and content creators, public shouldn't have access through the means of downgrading.
